Vegetable Section

Discover a wide variety of fresh, organic vegetables sourced directly from local farms. Our selection includes everything from leafy greens to root vegetables, ensuring you have access to the best produce for your meals. At AGROSTAR, we prioritize quality and sustainability, bringing you vegetables that are not only delicious but also grown with care for the environment.

Leafy Greens

Fresh and nutritious leafy greens including spinach, kale, and lettuce. Perfect for salads, smoothies, and more.

Root Vegetables

Organic root vegetables such as carrots, beets, and potatoes. Ideal for roasting, soups, and stews.

Cruciferous Vegetables

Healthy cruciferous vegetables like broccoli, cauliflower, and Brussels sprouts. Great for steaming, stir-frying, and more.
